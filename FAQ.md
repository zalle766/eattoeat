### What is Eat to Eat?
Eat to Eat is a comprehensive food delivery platform that connects customers with local restaurants and couriers. It provides a seamless experience for ordering food, tracking deliveries, and managing restaurant operations.

### Who can use Eat to Eat?
Eat to Eat is designed for four main user types:
- **Customers:** Order food from local restaurants
- **Restaurant Owners:** Manage their restaurant and orders
- **Couriers:** Accept and deliver food orders
- **Administrators:** Manage the entire platform

### Is Eat to Eat free to use?
- **Customers:** Free to use, only pay for food and delivery
- **Restaurants:** Commission-based pricing model
- **Couriers:** Free to join, earn per delivery
- **Administrators:** Platform management tools included

- Cash on delivery

### How does billing work for restaurants?
Restaurants pay a commission fee per order, typically 15-20% of the order value.

### How do couriers get paid?
Couriers receive payment per delivery, with rates varying by distance and order value.
